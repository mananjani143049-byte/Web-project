Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a package?

You can book via the Book button on any package, or use the Contact form to request a call back.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellation terms vary by package. Most packages allow cancellation with partial refund up to a number of days before departure — check the package T&Cs.

Do you provide travel insurance?

We can arrange travel insurance through trusted partners as an add-on.

Can I modify an itinerary?

Yes — many itineraries are customizable. Contact us with your preferences.
